\section{Circuits}%
\label{sec:Circuits}

Energy stored in a capacitor,
\[
U = \frac{Q^2}{2C} = \frac{1}{2}CV^2 = \frac{1}{2}QV
.\] 

Energy density (energy stored per unit volume in an electric field),

\[
U = \frac{1}{2}\varepsilon E^2
.\] 
Note: multiply this by the volume to find the amount of energy stored in that volume of the electric field.
